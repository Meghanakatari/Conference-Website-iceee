International Conference on Electrical Engineering and Electronics Engineering

ICEEEE-2024

21-23 Dec 2024, Rajahmundry, Andhra Pradesh, India


About Conference


The International Conference on Electrical Engineering and Electronics Engineering is scheduled for Dec 21–23, 2024, at the Godavari Institute of Engineering and Technology (A), Rajahmundry (AP), India. This international conference aims to foster communication and exchange of knowledge about current trends and approaches in Electrical, Electronics, and Communication Engineering among researchers, academicians, professionals from the industry, and students. The attendees will also learn about the various career options, current and planned technological developments, and applications in the fields of electrical, electronics, and communication engineering. These will be thoroughly discussed in the papers along with new research findings, and it is encouraged for new concepts and products to be submitted and presented.


About GIET(A)


Godavari Institute of Engineering and Technology(A), Rajahmundry (AP). GIET was established in the year 1998 to offer quality engineering education. It is located in the serene suburbs of Chaitanya Knowledge City, Rajahmundry on NH-16, the sprawling 300-acres of GIET campus. GIET is accredited by NAAC ‘A+’ Grade with CGPA 3.37 and NIRF ranked since 2019 consecutively. It is also recognized by the University Grants Commission 2(f) and 12(B) and is permanently affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK). GIET has collaborations with many international organizations.
